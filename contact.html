Frequently asked

How long does shipping take?+

Orders within Dhaka typically arrive in 1–2 business days. Outside Dhaka, delivery takes 3–5 business days depending on courier availability.

Are your products cruelty-free?+

Yes — every Porshella formula is cruelty-free and none of our products are tested on animals at any stage of development.

What's your return policy?+

Unopened products can be returned within 7 days of delivery. If something arrives damaged, reach out and we'll sort out a replacement right away.

Do you offer wholesale or bulk orders?+

We do. Select "Wholesale / partnership" in the contact form above and our team will follow up with pricing and minimum order details.
